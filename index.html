About Me

Welcome to my Portfolio, find a bit of everything from content videos, Graphic posters , blogs and to some really cool marketing campaings. I am someone who loves, breathes and shits content. I’ll admit, I’m no different from any other fresher with a WordPress or Wattpad blog they call a "portfolio" and a few college assignments squeezed into their resume.

But I knew marketing and social media were my thing when I worked with a D2C marketing agency this summer and learned the ropes from the ground up—ideating, scripting, and even shooting fun videos. (Not a marketing grad but take one bet on this econ grad).

Here’s what I bring to the table as a fresher: a promise to hold myself accountable—not just to do the job, but to stay curious. I’ll learn fast. Build fast. And I’ll give it everything I’ve got.

Primary Research Report

Primary research on the Local Action Plan for Climate Change in Pune City, with a focus on sustainable agriculture under the NAPCC Act. Analysed survey data from 159 households in the Hadapsar-Mundhwa ward using Stata, emphasizing economic impacts, resource allocation efficiency, and cost-effectiveness of sustainable practices.
